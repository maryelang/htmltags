About Me

I'm Marielle Anne B. Sumonod, an IT student at USTP and an elected SK Kagawad of Barangay 6. Passion drives me in everything I do, from organizing community events to mastering IT concepts. Cooking, music, and gaming are my cherished escapes. Whether crafting delicious dishes, losing myself in melodies, or immersing in virtual worlds, each activity brings joy and fulfillment. But above all, serving others is my greatest passion. As an SK Kagawad, I merge my love for technology with community service, striving to make a positive impact. Through dedication and compassion, I aim to leave a lasting mark on both the academic and civic realms.
